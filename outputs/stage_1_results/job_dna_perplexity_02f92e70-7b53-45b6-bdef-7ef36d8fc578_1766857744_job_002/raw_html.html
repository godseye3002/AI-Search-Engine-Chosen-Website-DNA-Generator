Mobile Measurement Partners: A Comparison

Get ready to take your mobile app marketing to the next level with our top picks for the best Mobile Measurement Partners (MMPs) in 2023! Don’t let the crowded market overwhelm you – we’ve done the research so you can make the right choice for your brand’s success.

How To Select Your Mobile Measurement Company

When selecting an MMP, consider factors such as compatibility with your tech stack, ad platform integrations, ability to measure last-touch and previous attributions, integration with reporting suites, SKadnetwork reporting abilities, ability to track the web-to-app journey, and tech and implementation support levels.

  • When selecting an MMP, consider the following questions:
    • Compatibility with your existing tech stack and whether the MMP integrates with your Customer Data Platform (CDP) to save time on development.
    • Ad platform and owned channel integrations to measure and de-dupe conversions across multiple channels.
    • Ability to measure last-touch and previous attributions to see which channel had an upper-funnel influence on the conversion.
    • Ability to integrate with reporting suites to report back to stakeholders without requiring custom data connectors.
    • SKadnetwork reporting abilities to identify the source of conversion, combine actual identified conversions, and push conversions back to ad platforms.
    • Ability to track the web-to-app journey to effectively attribute users who come from an external source and land on the website, download the app, and make a purchase.
    • Tech and implementation support levels to ensure everything works correctly from the start.

In this list, I’ll introduce you to eight top mobile measurement partners: Adjust, AppsFlyer, Branch, Kochava, Airbridge, RevenueCat, Singular, and Tenjin. Each of these partners offers unique features to help you track user behavior and understand your marketing campaign’s performance.

With Adjust, you can examine store statistics, advertising sources, and advanced analytics to fully monetize your application.Adjust is the choice of established brands such as Yelp, Zynga, Spotify, Soundcloud, and Runtastic1. Adjust is an end-to-end solution for every stage of the app marketing journey. You can measure, optimize, and scale app growth across platforms with their award-winning technology.They also offer a smart alerts solution called Pulse to boost efficiency3.
